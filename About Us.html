Nugagahgedara Institute


The Nugagahagedara Institute was started by Annoor youth club in 2001. This educational institution was started only for the higher grade divisions of science stream and commerce stream. Later in 2013 the technology stream was introduced by the education ministry of Sri Lanka. Therefore classes for technology was also conducted in this institution.

In our Nugagahagedara institute, we not only provide the educational knowledge for the advanced level students, we also conduct motivational programs, job guidance programs and also for new invention guidance programs. Moreover we also keep health fit sports to maintain a proper health state, and we hold religious programs in order to build a healthy society in the future. By considering the students future, we conduct classes with the help of famous and expert teachers from Sri Lanka. Not only that we also keep unit tests subject wise, unit wise seminars are held and past papers, model papers are done for the students. So in this way prepare the students for the final exams for 100%.

The students from our Nugagahagedara institute have achieved island first, district first from science, commerce and technology stream. Specially more than hundreds of students have entered universities, colleges and also continuing their higher studies in various higher educational institutions. Moreover our students from Nugagahagedara institute have invented new inventions, many are working in higher organizations and thus they have kept records in various fields. Advance level science stream, commerce stream, technology stream in Tamil medium classes are conducted in Nugagahagedara institute which is famous national due to its specialty. So the advanced level students can join our institute and make your future simple.
